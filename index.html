Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data from our app, please follow these instructions:

  1. Send an email to support@yourdomain.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your username and the email address associated with your account in the body of the email.
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and notify you once your data has been deleted.

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
